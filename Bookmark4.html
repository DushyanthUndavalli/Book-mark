Rashmika

Rashmika Mandanna (/rəʃmɪkɑː mənðənɑː/; born 5 April 1996) is an Indian actress who works in Telugu, Kannada, Hindi and Tamil films. She has received four SIIMA Awards and a Filmfare Award. One of South India's highest-paid actresses, she was featured in Forbes India's 2024 list of "30 Under 30"


Kirtishetty

Krithi Shetty (born 21 September 2003)[1] is an Indian actress who predominantly works in Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam and Hindi films. She made her debut with the commercially successful film Uppena (2021). She established herself as one of the leading actresses in Telugu cinema.


Sreeleela

Sreeleela (alternatively spelt as Sree Leela;[1][2] born June 14, 2001) is an American actress of Indian origin who predominantly works in Telugu and Kannada cinema. She began her career as a child artist in 2017, before playing a leading role in the 2019 Kannada film Kiss, and then starred in the Telugu films Pelli SandaD (2021) and Dhamaka (2022). The lattermost won her the SIIMA Award for Best Actress – Telugu. In 2023 and 2024, she had four critical and commercial failures in Skanda, Aadikeshava, Extra Ordinary Man, and Guntur Kaaram.
